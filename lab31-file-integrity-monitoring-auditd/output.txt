-----------------------------------------

-----------------------------------------
Notes (Learner Reflection)
-----------------------------------------
- Monitoring sensitive files like /etc/passwd, /etc/shadow, /etc/sudoers, and /etc/ssh/sshd_config
  helps detect persistence or privilege escalation attempts.
- Audit keys (like passwd_changes) simplify searching and reporting.
